Slype.

A data-aware, browser-based slideshow framework

Slype is a slideshow framework.

As you'd expect, it handles text slides with aplomb. Layouts are determined by CSS, so style is separated from content: you're free to focus on what you're saying, and recycle styles and formats.

Slype has two-column layouts.

And sub-headings, too.
